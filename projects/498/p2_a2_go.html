a2go (Unity Engine)

Description

For this project, my partner, Frank Wang, and I created a Pokemon Go clone, re-themed for the city of Ann Arbor, to encourage visitors to explore the city. Players can learn about the city by walking around to discover different landmarks. Discovering a new landmark unlocks a new tree seed in their shop. Fully-grown trees generate coins which can be used to buy more tree seeds. In the Ann Arbor spirit, the game also has squirrels. A squirrel standing by a tree will prevent the tree from quickly generating coins. The user can enter AR Mode to launch acorns at the squirrels to try and appease them. They can also place a fence around any tree to prevent the squirrels from bugging that tree.

Features

  • Two gameplay modes: Interaction Mode (AR) and Exploration Mode (Map), with different music to set the tone
  • Orbit camera behavior around the player's avatar when in Exploration Mode
  • Persistently north-facing compass to keep the user oriented as they explore the world
  • 6 different tree types, ranging from realistic to imagined, which generate coins once fully grown
  • Plant trees in AR and watch them grow
  • Scrollable inventory bar to keep track of seed, acorn, and fence counts
  • Shop menu to buy more seeds, acorns, and fences
  • Squirrels which spawn at random intervals to prevent trees from generating coins
  • Appeasing squirrels with acorns generates DogeCoin, which can be used to buy fences to protect your trees
  • Currency boost for spotting the Ann Arbor emblem

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine
  • Blender
  • Audacity
  • JIRA
