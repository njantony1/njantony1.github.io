CSE Simulator (Unreal Engine)

Description

For this project, my partner, Frank Wang, and I were tasked with recreating one of the computer labs in the BBB at the University of Michigan. This project is created with Unreal Engine in VR and includes some extra fun twists which Michigan students in particular might find fun.

Features

  • Desks with computers, room dividers, support pillars
  • Materials for the carpet, walls, ceiling, and grass
  • Light switches with two zones to turn on/off the lights
  • One flickering light which makes a sound with each flicker
  • Background ambiance of people working
  • A whiteboard which you can draw on
  • Copy machines which duplicate objects
  • Posters advertising EECS project instructor solutions when you click on them
  • Pieces of trash left by lazy, tired students
  • Trash cans which you can use to throw out items
  • A fire alarm which plays an alarm sound and flashes the lights red
  • 3 NPCs with unique dialogue: Mario, Toad, and a Penguin
  • Day/night progression when you interact with an object
  • Mark Zuckerberg taunting the students by dancing outside of the windows
  • A door to exit the simulation for when you are sufficiently tired

Technologies Used

  • Unreal Engine
  • Blender
  • Audacity
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • JIRA
  • git
